About

Drawing inspiration from Zyte's books.toscrape.com, we offer a dynamic and evolving platform that mimicks the dynamics of a music industry. You can use our website and API to learn how to scrape. Built by Tilburg Science Hub for educational purposes, we hope this platform helps students and data science enthousiasts around the world to learn scraping without the usual (legal) boundaries.

Exploring Boundaries

We've simulated an entire universe of music, artists, and users, creating a dynamic playground for extracting data from our platform. Our data, initialized with a list of artists and songs retrieved from Mahkaila's GitHub repository, encompasses a rich catalog of artists and songs. However, the magic lies in how users interact with the content, producing a captivating array of consumption behaviors.

Explore popular artists, discover emerging trends, and understand user preferences as you delve into the interplay between music and data. Our platform presents you with a comprehensive API, opening doors to endless possibilities for analysis, visualization, and innovation.

Join the Open Source Movement

We believe in the power of collaboration and learning through hands-on experience. Our project is entirely open source, and we invite you to be a part of our journey. Contribute, experiment, and shape the future of web scraping and data exploration by joining our community on GitHub. Explore our repository at github.com/tilburgsciencehub/music-to-scrape and make your mark.

Your Privacy Matters

As advocates of responsible data usage, we take your privacy seriously. We've outlined our privacy practices and usage terms in our dedicated Privacy & Terms section. Feel free to explore this section to understand how we handle data, ensure your security, and create a safe environment for exploration.
